　　在用英语交流的过程中，不论是想质疑朋友对一场篮球比赛的预测结果，还是要告知商务合作伙伴你对他们的销售预估量存有疑虑，你都可以试用以下八道题的正确答案来礼貌、委婉地表达自己的不同看法。做小测验，学习八个用来发出质疑、表示顾虑的语句。　　　　　　a) I agree.　　b) I agree up to a point.　　c) I disagree.　　d) That isn’t true.　　　　　　a) I don’t follow that argument.　　b) I’d go along with that.　　c) I agree to an extent.　　d) It’s hard to disagree.　　　　a) I don’t know whether I’d entirely agree.　　b) I don’t believe that’s true.　　c) I don’t see it, myself.　　d) I believe what you say.　　　　a) But don’t you think that he has a right to know about this?　　b) Perhaps, but that may not be all there is to it.　　c) Well, possibly, but I think it will work out fine.　　d) I’m afraid that’s not how I see it.　　　　a) I take your point　　b) I take your idea　　c) I take your thought　　d) I take your statement　　　　　　a) You’re totally wrong!　　b) You could be right, but...　　c) You must be right.　　d) That’s a fair point.　　　　a) Do you really think so?　　b) That sounds about right, but I wonder if she sees it differently.　　c) Not necessarily.　　d) Sorry, I can’t accept that.　　　　a) I have no idea what you mean!　　b) I understand you perfectly.　　c) I mostly agree, but could you explain a bit more.　　d) I couldn’t disagree more.　　　　1) b, 2) c, 3) a, 4) d, 5) a, 6) b, 7) d, 8) c.　　实习编辑：姚依明　　责任编辑：潘程
