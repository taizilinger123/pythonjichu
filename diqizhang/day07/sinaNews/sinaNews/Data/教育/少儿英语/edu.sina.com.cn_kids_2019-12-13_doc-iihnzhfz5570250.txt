　　在用英语发表个人观点时，要注意表达清晰，语气礼貌。怎样才能既准确又不失礼貌地表达自己的想法、观点？英语中有哪些固定表达可以帮助语言学习者达到这一目的？做测验题，学习表达个人观点时可以使用的礼貌用语。　　　　a) Let’s see if　　b) If you see it like this　　c) My colleague said　　d) As far as I’m concerned　　　　a) experience　　b) duration　　c) time　　d) existence　　　　a) opinion　　b) seeing　　c) view　　d) looking　　　　a) In my opinion　　b) In my brain　　c) In my ideas　　d) In my frame of mind　　　　a) To my thoughts　　b) To my mind　　c) To my vision　　d) To my psychology　　　　a) From my stance　　b) From my head　　c) From my place　　d) From my perspective　　　　a) I deliver the opinion that graffiti adds character to a city.　　b) I hold the opinion that graffiti adds character to a city.　　c) I control the opinion that graffiti adds character to a city.　　d) I handle the opinion that graffiti adds character to a city.　　　　a) There is no doubt in my mind that Italy is by far the best country in Europe for cuisine.　　b) I reckon Italy is by far the best country in Europe for cuisine.　　c) In my opinion, Italy is the best country in Europe for cuisine.　　d) For my experience, Italy is the best country in Europe for cuisine.　　　　1) d, 2) a, 3) c, 4) a, 5) b, 6) d, 7) b, 8) d.　　实习编辑：姚依明　　责任编辑：潘程
